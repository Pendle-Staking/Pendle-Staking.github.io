What is Pendle Staking?

Pendle is a DeFi yield-trading protocol; its sPENDLE module lets users stake PENDLE 1:1 for governance power and eligible reward distributions, as described in the official sPENDLE docs.

How Pendle Staking works

  1. Open the appUse the official Pendle V2 route and confirm the domain is app.pendle.finance before connecting a wallet.
  2. Connect walletConnect the wallet holding PENDLE on Ethereum and review token approvals before signing.
  3. Stake PENDLEEnter the amount, approve if needed, and confirm the transaction to receive sPENDLE at a 1:1 ratio.
  4. Manage positionUse the dashboard to vote when required, claim eligible rewards, or unstake through the standard withdrawal period or instant fee option.

Before you stake

Built for sPENDLE

Pendle’s current staking flow centers on sPENDLE, while vePENDLE is legacy and being phased out in favor of the newer governance and reward token model.

Active participation matters

Reward eligibility depends on the rules in each epoch, including participation when a Pendle Protocol Proposal is available; the app is the source for current status.

Clear exit paths

sPENDLE can be unstaked back to PENDLE after a 14-day withdrawal period, or immediately with the fee described in the official documentation.

Pendle Staking FAQ

What is pendle staking?

pendle staking is the process of staking PENDLE into sPENDLE through the official Pendle app for governance participation and eligible reward distributions.

Where do I start pendle staking?

Start only from app.pendle.finance, then verify the connected network, token approval, and transaction details before signing.

Does pendle staking have a lock?

The sPENDLE docs state that standard unstaking returns PENDLE 1:1 after a 14-day withdrawal period, with an immediate unstake option that carries a 5% fee.

How are pendle staking rewards handled?

Pendle allocates a portion of V2 fees to PENDLE buybacks, and eligible active sPENDLE holders may claim distributions through the dApp; no fixed APY is guaranteed here.

Is pendle staking the same as vePENDLE?

No. vePENDLE is legacy in the current documentation, while sPENDLE is the active staking, governance, and reward model users are directed toward.
